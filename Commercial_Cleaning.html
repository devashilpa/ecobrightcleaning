Commercial Cleaning Services

EcoBright delivers professional commercial cleaning solutions designed to keep your business clean, safe, and welcoming. From offices and daycare centers to medical facilities, Airbnb properties, and newly renovated spaces, our experienced team provides reliable, high-quality cleaning services tailored to your operational needs.

  • General Cleaning Services: Comprehensive cleaning for commercial properties, including dusting, vacuuming, mopping, restroom cleaning, and waste removal.
  • Office Cleaning: Maintain a clean, organized, and productive workplace with scheduled cleaning for offices, meeting rooms, workstations, and common areas.
  • Daycare & Medical Cleaning: Specialized cleaning and sanitization for childcare centers and healthcare facilities using industry-approved practices.
  • Common Area Maintenance: Keep lobbies, hallways, elevators, stairwells, break rooms, and shared spaces clean and presentable.
  • Airbnb Cleaning: Fast and reliable turnover cleaning, linen changes, restocking essentials, and guest-ready preparation for short-term rental properties.
  • Post-Construction / Post-Renovation Cleaning: Thorough removal of dust, debris, and construction residue to prepare newly completed spaces for occupancy.
  • Sanitization Services: Professional disinfection of high-touch surfaces and shared areas to help maintain a healthier environment for employees and visitors.
